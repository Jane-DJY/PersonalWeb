Projects

The Lighthouse: Shining a Light on the Gender Gap in Economic Engagement

This video visualizes the structural roots of gender disparities in global investment through a lighthouse model. By mapping economic, legal, and educational data from around the world, it reveals how foundational systems shape women's access to investment and financial autonomy.

Twin Cloud- Using Atmospheric Data Visualization to Depict the Breath of Cities

Clouds mirror the ground's activity‚Äîvolcanoes, wars, and forests all imprint the sky. Twin Cloud uses global meteorological data to generate clouds that reflect local conditions. Sulfur dioxide signals industry; sea salt suggests coastal traits. As pollution grows, clouds shift from gentle to violent.

Stock Forest: Revealing the Evolving Stories of the Stock Market through Visualization

This video explores the evolution of the US stock market through multi-dimensional data mapping, using tree diagrams to represent each listed company. By analyzing characteristics such as tree color, height, and width, we delve into various market dynamics, including company health, market valuation, and industry concentration.

Looking Back at 72 Years of Asian Games: How Did Women's Sports Get Here?

This work analyzes the medal counts for men and women across different events in the past eighteen Asian Games. In the first Asian Games, women were only allowed to participate in track and field events. By the 2010 Guangzhou Asian Games, all major events were finally open to women for the first time!

Your Feeling is Right: Movies Are Indeed Getting Longer

Through IMDB data analysis, we discovered that popular movies are indeed gradually increasing in length, especially in the years following the pandemic. Moreover, longer movies receive substantial financial investments, with high star salaries and astonishing marketing costs, with most resources concentrated on just a few films.

Who runs China?

This project introduces the composition characteristics of the 13th National People's Congress (NPC) representatives in China, analyzing their gender, age, ethnicity, education, and professional background through data and images. It also extends to the evolution of the "Two Sessions" report content, reflecting changes in China's political system operation and national governance priorities.
